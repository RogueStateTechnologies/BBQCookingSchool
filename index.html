Teaching techniques, not recipes

Who is this course for?
The Master BBQ School has been written to greatly improve the skills of all BBQ enthusiasts from the backyard barbequer to professional chefs or competitive cooks.
What does the course teach?
The Master BBQ School is an eight-lesson correspondence course that you study at home using your own cooking equipment and at your own pace. The only equipment needed is a grill and a dedicated smoker. The course will teach you the core techniques need to master BBQ cooking, such as, direct and indirect grilling and classic wood-smoked BBQ. The course also teaches how to judge and choose different cuts of meat. After you've gone through the course you will have all the tools and knowledge you need to start experimenting with different smoke woods, brines, rubs, and sauces to perfect your own styles and flavors.
What assistance is offered to students?
John is always available to answer questions and assist students in making this course a truly terrific experience. Students that complete the course within one calender year from the time they send us the first lesson will be awarded a Master BBQ Cook Certificate and a Letter of Graduation.
American Culinary Federation (ACF) certified chefs will also have earned 50 hours of Continuing Education Credit toward re-certification.

What you'll learn

  1. learning the basics about your equipment and how it operates and the meat you'll be cooking
